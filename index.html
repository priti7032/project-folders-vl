Explore the climate of Goa - temperature, rainfall, and seasons.

Temperature in Goa

Goa experiences a tropical climate with warm temperatures throughout the year.

  • Summer (March to May): 25°C to 35°C
  • Monsoon (June to September): 23°C to 30°C
  • Winter (October to February): 20°C to 30°C

Seasons in Goa

Goa has three main seasons:

  1. Summer
  2. Monsoon
  3. Winter

    4. Rainfall in Goa

    Goa receives heavy rainfall during the monsoon season.

    • Average annual rainfall: 3000 mm
    • Monsoon months: June to September
    • Peak rainfall in July and August

Each season has its own unique climate characteristics and activities.
