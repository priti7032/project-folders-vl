Tourism and Climate in Goa

Goa's climate plays a significant role in its tourism industry.

The peak tourist season is during the cooler and drier months from November to February.

The monsoon season (June to September) sees fewer tourists due to heavy rainfall, but it is a great time for nature lovers and those seeking a quieter experience.

Many festivals and events are scheduled around the climate seasons to attract visitors.

Tourism businesses adapt their offerings based on the climate, providing seasonal activities and experiences.
